STRUCTURE 
RCC framed structure to with 
stand wind & seismic Loads.
SUPER STRUCTURE 
6” thick CC blocks for external walls and 
4” thick CC blocks for internal walls.
Internal : 1 coat of plastering in CM for walls.
External : 2 coat of plastering in CM for 
External walls.FLOORING 
Living, dining, master bedroom and 
other bedrooms.
Floor Tiles : Aparna or equivalent.
Kitchen Areas : Vitrified tiles of reputed make.
Balcony : Premium ceramic tiles of reputed 
make with matt finish.
Bathrooms : Acid resistant, anti-skid 
ceramic tiles of reputed make.
Corridors : Vitrified tiles of reputed make.
Stair Case : Cementineous step tile.
Utilities : Ceramic tiles of reputed make.ELECTRICAL 
Power outlets for geysers and exhaust fans in 
all bathrooms. Power plug for cooking range, 
chimney, refrigerator, microwave ovens, mixer /
grinders in kitchen.
Distribution boards and MCB’s of reputed make.
Modular electrical switches of reputed make.
Power outlets for air conditioners in living and 
all bedrooms.
Switches : Crabtree or equivalent.
DOORS 
Main Door : Engineered wood frame & designer 
teak veneered shutter with reputed hardware 
fittings with both sides polish finishing.
Internal Doors : Engineered wood frame & 
designer veneered / laminate shutter with 
reputed hardware fittings.
Bathroom / Utility : Engineered hard wood frame 
& Designer veneered / laminate shutter or ABS 
frames and shutter.
French doors : UPVC door frames with float 
glass with provision for mosquito mesh.
Windows : Winster or equivalent. 
UPVC windows with provision for mosquito 
mesh (Provision of track for mosquito mesh 
will be as per Window type and feasibility). 
Mosquito mesh & installation for windows 
and french door shall be at extra cost).
Grills : Aesthetically designed, MS grills with 
enamel paint finish. (only for staircase and 
balconies).PAINTING 
Internal : Smooth putty finish with 2 coats of 
premium acrylic emulsion paint of reputed make 
over a coat of primer.
External : Textured / luppum finish and two coats 
of exterior emulsion paint of reputed make.TELECOM / CABLE TV / INTERNET 
Telephone provision in MBR & drawing area. 
Provision for internet connection in each flat. 
Provision for cable connection in 
Master bedroom & living room.
WTP & STP 
Domestic water made available through an 
exclusive water softening plant. 
A sewage treatment plant of adequate capacity 
as per norms will be provided inside the project, 
treated sewage water will be used for the 
landscaping and flushing purpose.BATHROOMS 
Walls mounted EWC in all bathrooms. Single 
lever diverter with shower of reputed make. 
All CP and sanitary fittings of reputed make. 
Kohler or equivalent.
LIFTS 
Each tower contains 2 passenger and 
1 service elevator. Kone or equivalent.KITCHEN 
Provision of fixing water purifier, 
Exhaust fan / chimney. 
Provision of washing machine in the utility area. 
Provision for granite platform and sink.POWER BACKUP 
100% DG backup for common areas and 1kv 
backup or lighting circuit in flats.
FIRE SAFETY 
Fire system will be provided as per 
fire department norms.S
