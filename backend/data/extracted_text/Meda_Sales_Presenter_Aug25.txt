Strong Foundations 
 Require Visionary 
Leadership.
A pioneering force in South India’s infrastructure 
and real estate landscape, Meda Raghunadha Reddy 
began his journey in 1987 with little more than vision 
and resolve. A first-generation entrepreneur, he built 
MRKR and MEDA Constructions on a foundation of 
engineering excellence, timely delivery, and enduring 
relationships.
Over the decades, his leadership has shaped 
₹13,000 Cr+ worth of infrastructure projects and 
delivered 2.8 million sq. ft. of residential and 
commercial spaces across cities like Bangalore, 
Hyderabad, and Tirupati. From irrigation works to 
landmark residences and an upcoming hospitality 
project in Tirupati, his portfolio is as expansive as his 
ambition.
Yet, it’s not just about scale. Raghunadha Reddy’s 
legacy lies in the values he champions - integrity, 
community upliftment, and environmental 
responsibility. A man of few words but deep conviction, 
he remains a mentor and guiding force, ensuring MEDA 
builds not just structures, but lives and legacies.
Raghunadha Reddy
